Who We Are

Heaven Base Technical Services is a leading provider of high-quality interior and fit-out solutions, specializing in Retail, Residential, Hospitality, and Family Entertainment Centers (FECs).

With a team of skilled professionals, we offer a comprehensive range of services including gypsum installation, cladding, partition works, painting finishes, ceiling works, joinery, MEP solutions, marble work, and specialized tiling services.

Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction ensures that every project is completed with precision and craftsmanship.
